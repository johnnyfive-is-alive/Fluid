About Products

Products represent work packages or programs that resources, stations, and units are allocated to.

Action Buttons:
  • View: See which items are mapped to this product and usage statistics
  • Enhanced NEW: Select specific items by characteristics (location, capacity, etc.) and assign percentage allocations across multiple months
  • Loading: View standard loading profile showing month-to-month requirements
  • Edit: Change the product name
  • Delete: Remove the product (only if not in use)
